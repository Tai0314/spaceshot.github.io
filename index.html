The Odyssey is about a man named Odysseus who goes through many challenges and adventures to get back home to Ithaca. With his crew, Odyssyes incounters many dangers such as a Cyclops, Sirens and Charybdis. He constanly has set backs and constanly has to keep his crew out of trouble. For example, some of Odysseus's men go to the home of Circe and she invites them in. After the men drink a potion that was given to them by Circe, they all turn into pigs. This made Odysseus come to Circe's home and make a deal with her to not drink or eat unless his men were turned back. Odysseus had to stay with Circe until she agreed to let him and Circe told Odysseus that going to the underworld will help him find directions to his home.
